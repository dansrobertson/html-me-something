This one time a member of the facility, I guess from the math department, wrote half an equation on a blackboard out in the main corridor. I was there mopping the floor late one night and noticed it. After looking over it for a while I was somehow able to figure it out. Anyway, Ben Affleck was my best friend at the time and we had some wicked adventures. "How about them apples?"

One time I was living out in the African safari, this is when I was a bit younger, just a small child. Anyway, I was hiking through the bottom of a big gorge, tall, tall cliffs on both sides, when I stopped to take a break in the shade. Then there was this weird rumbling and the ground was kind of shaking and I thought it might be an earthquake. When I looked up I saw a huge herd of wildebeest charging down the slope into the gorge, I guess they got spooked by some hyenas. I thought I was going to die clinging on the tree branch above the stampede, but right before it broke my dad came charging in and pulled me out and threw me to safety. He had to climb a different way to get out, and he almost made it to my uncle that was reaching out to help him but i guess he just couldn't hold on and he feel under the hooves of all those beasts.
